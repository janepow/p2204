Meet the Team

We are a team of highly motivated GIS students, currently enrolled in the GIS Application Specialist program at Fleming College. We are bringing information and spatial analysis to the world, one project at a time.

Justin Bassard

GIS Application Specialist

Justin is currently enrolled in the GIS Applications Specialist program at Fleming College. He graduated from McMaster University with a B.A in Geography and a Concurrent certificate in GIS. He aspires to do his Masters in GIS the upcoming years and hope to continue to expand his skillset in the industry.

Lianne Chu

GIS Application Specialist

A graduate of the Geophysics program at Queen's University, Lianne is currently a candidate for the GIS Application Sepcialist program at Fleming College. She has worked for two summers as a GIS technician and data analyst with the Ontario Government, a GIS technician and data coordinator at Pipetek Infrastructure Services, and a data coordinator at the City of Kingston. Although learning about the scientific theories that much of today's GIS technology is built upon was facinicating, Lianne's love of designing maps and diagrams using GIS software are the reasons she chose a career in GIS.
